Rules

1- At the begining of the game,
put all cards face against table

2- The player puts successively 2 cards face
up, here the cards are differents

3- As the two cards are differents, the
player hides the cards again.

4- Is now the turn of the player to put face
up two card again, the cards are the same,
the player found the two cards of the pair.

5- As the cards are the same, the player win
them and play again and put face up two
other cards...
